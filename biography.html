Biography

My name is Toni Clark, I am currently living the state of North Carolina where both the beach and the mountains are just in arms distance away. I enjoy hiking, traveling, and anything that could lead to a grand new adventure. As much as I love being outside, you are just as likely to find me inside with a good book or movie spending the day on the couch with some type of dessert attempting to bake in the oven.

I have bounced around state to state all my life. Starting in Kansas, moving to Washington State, North Carolina, then Florida and back. Moving to these widely different places has shown me a lot about the ways people can live. It can also give you every accent and no accent at all so when you talk to people they just get confused. My favorite place to live by far would have to be Washington State.
